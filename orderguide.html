Quantity available for purchase

Dr. Jart+'s official online mall has limits on the number of purchases per day and the number of orders. (Purchase available: 3 times per day per ID, maximum purchase quantity per day is 14, single item is 8) If the order cancellation is completed, the number of purchases is restored.
